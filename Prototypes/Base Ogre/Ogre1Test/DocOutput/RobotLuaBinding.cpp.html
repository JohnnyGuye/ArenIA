turnRobot(angle:double):void

Description

Gives the robot the order to turn at a certain angle

Parameters

  • angle:double The angle in degree that the robot has to turn. A positive angle means he will turn anti-clockwise, and a negative one means he will turn anti-clockwise.

example

How to turn

    function main()
        turn(50)
        -- Now the robot will turn in order to have a angle of 50 degres
    end

turnTurret(angle:double):void

Description

Gives the robot the order to turn his turret at a certain angle

Parameters

  • angle:double The angle in degree that the turret has to turn. A positive angle means he will turn anti-clockwise, and a negative one means he will turn anti-clockwise.
